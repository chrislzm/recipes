Tools:
 - Sous vide setup (machine, tub, water)
 - Vacuum sealer setup (machine, bags)

Ingrediets
 - Olive oil
 - Chicken breast

Instructions
 - Preheat sous vide to 150 degrees F
 - Add chicken breast to vacuum seal bag
 - Add 1/2 tsp olive oil to vacuum seal bag
 - Seal the bag
 - Submerge bag in preheated water for 1 hour
 - Done!
